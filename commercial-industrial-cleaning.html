Commercial & Industrial

Heavy-Duty Facility Care

Philosophy

Built for the Demands of Heavy Industry.

Manufacturing plants, engineering facilities, and heavy commercial spaces present unique, high-intensity challenges. Standard cleaning methods simply cannot cope with the heavy grease, industrial dust, and vast floor spaces characteristic of these environments.

Our industrial division deploys specialist machinery, highly potent (yet compliant) degreasing agents, and rigorously trained operatives. We focus on maintaining optimal hygiene and safety without ever compromising your operational uptime or disrupting your production schedules.

Methodology

Specialized Industrial Capabilities

Equipped to handle the toughest grime, grease, and scale with minimal disruption to your daily operations.

01.

Machine Scrubbing

We deploy high-capacity ride-on and walk-behind scrubber dryers to rapidly deep clean concrete and epoxy floors, removing dangerous slip hazards and ingrained dirt.

02.

Production Line Sanitation

Careful, methodical cleaning of sensitive manufacturing zones and assembly lines, scheduled meticulously during your planned downtime to ensure zero impact on output.

03.

High-Level Cleaning

Safe extraction of combustible dust and debris from high structural steelwork, pipework, lighting, and HVAC systems using MEWPs and specialized vacuum arrays.

04.

Exterior & Loading Bays

Industrial pressure washing for loading bays, service yards, and heavy-traffic exterior zones to remove tire marks, oil spills, and heavy weathering.

05.

Welfare & Amenity Zones

Maintaining hygiene and morale by thoroughly sanitizing high-traffic factory environments, including staff canteens, locker rooms, and communal washrooms.

06.

Waste & Spill Control

Organized removal of industrial waste from production areas and safe management of minor chemical or oil spills in strict adherence to site protocols.

Industrial Compliance

Operating around heavy machinery and industrial processes demands a zero-compromise approach to Health and Safety. We integrate fully into your existing HSE frameworks.

Site-Specific RAMS

Detailed Risk Assessments and Method Statements provided prior to commencing any work in a commercial or industrial setting.

COSHH Trained Staff

All operatives undergo strict COSHH training for the safe handling, deployment, and storage of industrial solvents and degreasers.

CSCS & IPAF Certified

Our teams carry valid CSCS cards for construction zones and IPAF certifications for operating mobile elevated work platforms.

Full PPE Adherence

Teams arrive fully equipped with site-specific high-visibility clothing, hard hats, safety footwear, and protective eyewear at all times.
