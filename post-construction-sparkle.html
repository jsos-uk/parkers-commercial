Post-Construction

From Site To Handover

Philosophy

Flawless Presentation for Final Handover.

The transition from a busy construction site to a pristine commercial or residential space requires meticulous attention to detail. Construction dust settles everywhere, and leftover paint splatters or protective films can completely ruin a client's first impression.

We partner with property developers, shopfitters, and main contractors to provide phased, heavy-duty cleaning. Our teams bridge the gap between building completion and final inspection, guaranteeing your project is handed over in absolutely immaculate condition.

Methodology

A Phased Approach to Perfection

We utilize a structured, multi-stage cleaning process to capture every speck of construction debris and dust.

01.

Initial Builders Clean

The first stage involves the extraction of heavy debris, removal of protective films and tape, and the initial suppression of bulk airborne dust.

02.

High-Level Dusting

Specialized removal of fine construction dust from exposed ductwork, cable trays, lighting rigs, and high structural beams.

03.

Window & Glazing Polish

Deep cleaning of interior and exterior glass panels, carefully removing stubborn render, paint flecks, and cement splatter without scratching.

04.

Hard Floor Restoration

Machine scrubbing and sealing of new hard floors, tiles, and concrete to remove persistent boot marks and prevent concrete dust blooming.

05.

Amenity Deep Clean

Detailed sanitization of newly installed washrooms, staff kitchens, and welfare areas, ensuring they are completely hygienic and ready for immediate use.

06.

The Final Sparkle

The last detailed polish—wiping down all remaining surfaces, shining chrome fixtures, and vacuuming to ensure absolute perfection before the client walk-through.
